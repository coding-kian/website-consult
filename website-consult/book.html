Scope rules

The price buys a defined output, not unlimited requests

Extra questions, extra assets, extra sites, extra datasets or ongoing support are quoted separately.

Terms

  • Each paid review starts with one agreed scope.
  • Unclear or incomplete evidence may delay delivery.
  • One clarification or revision round is included unless agreed otherwise.
  • Monitoring only covers the agreed watchlist or risk question.
  • Reports are evidence reviews, not financial, legal, tax or investment advice.
