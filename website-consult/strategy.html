Strategy

The public version of the approach

This page shows the shape of the review without exposing the research method. The detailed framework sits behind the audit process.

Review pipeline

From claim to decision output

Claim→ Entity→ Features→ Evidence views→ Structure→ Drift→ Decision output

1. Define the claim

What is being relied on: value, usage, infrastructure, adoption, resilience or risk.

2. Test the evidence

Compare the claim against observed signals and practical constraints.

3. Report the risk

Show what holds, what breaks, what is uncertain and what needs checking next.

Bridge logic + evidence views

Digital activity creates infrastructure pressure

Crypto, property and AI infrastructure connect through one pattern: activity creates demand, demand meets scarce capacity, and concentration changes value, risk and timing.

market usage development infrastructure geography 
activity → demand → constraint
constraint → concentration
concentration → value + risk + drift

Signal checks

Enough detail to trust the process. Not enough to copy it

The exact evidence and weighting depend on the review scope. The report is not based on one isolated metric.

Example checks

  • Neighbour overlap: do different evidence views point to the same risk group?
  • Drift distance: is the structure stable, moving or breaking down?
